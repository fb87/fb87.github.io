Then a man said: Speak...

Then a man said: Speak to us of Expectations.
He then said: If a man does not see or hear the waters of the

Jordan, then he should not taste the pomegranate or ply his wares in an open market. If a man would not labour in the salt and rock quarries then he should not accept of the Earth that which he refuses to give of himself.

Such a man would expect a pear of a peach tree.
Such a man would expect a stone to lay an egg.
Such a man would expect Sears to assemble a lawnmower.
	-- Kehlog Albran, "The Profit"
